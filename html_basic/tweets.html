Sunsets are one of nature’s most beautiful phenomena. The science behind sunsets is fascinating. Sunsets occur when the sun dips below the horizon and its light passes through more of the Earth’s atmosphere. This causes the light to scatter in different directions, creating the beautiful colors we see in the sky. source:blog.education.nationalgeographic
